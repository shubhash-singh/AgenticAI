Projectile Motion Simulator

Basic projectile motion with angle and speed. Understand how angle affects range and observe the effect of initial speed on maximum height.

Angle and Range: The range of a projectile is affected by the launch angle. A higher angle results in a longer range.
Initial Speed and Maximum Height: The initial speed of a projectile affects its maximum height. A higher initial speed results in a higher maximum height.
In conclusion, the projectile motion simulator demonstrates how angle and initial speed affect the range and maximum height of a projectile.
